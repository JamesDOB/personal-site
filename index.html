001 — About

I make things work
for real people.

Not in a theoretical, deck-slide kind of way. I mean the kind of work where you talk to users until you actually understand what's broken, build the thing, watch it land, and iterate until it's right.

My background cuts across community building, product strategy, and AI tooling — which sounds like a weird combo until you realize they're all about the same thing: figuring out what people need and building systems that meet them there.

Before I got into tech, I was an ELA teacher in Brooklyn. That chapter taught me more about engagement, trust, and what makes people actually show up than any product framework has. I bring that lens everywhere.

Right now I'm deep in AI-enabled approaches to learning — not the hype, the practical stuff. Can AI analyze a tutoring session and give real coaching feedback? Yes. I built it.
